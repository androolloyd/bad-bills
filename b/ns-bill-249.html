Distribution of Fake Intimate Images Act

Sponsor: Suzy Hansen (NDP) — private member's bill · Status: Private member's bill (before the House) · Nova Scotia · Provincial

Would amend the Intimate Images and Cyber-protection Act to cover AI-generated and deepfake intimate images.

✅ The case for

Closes a real gap as deepfake abuse rises; protects victims.

⚠️ The case against

As a private member's bill it is less likely to advance without government support.

🔐 Privacy & civil liberties: Rights-positive — strengthens protection against non-consensual and AI-faked intimate images.

👥 Who it affects

Victims of image-based and deepfake abuse.
