Build Canada Homes

Sponsor: Government bill · Status: In committee · Federal · Parliament of Canada

Stands up a federal housing agency to boost supply, part of the government's housing push.

✅ The case for

Aimed squarely at the housing-affordability crisis.

⚠️ The case against

Questions about execution, cost and how fast it delivers.

🔐 Privacy & civil liberties: Minimal.

👥 Who it affects

Renters and would-be buyers.

✔️ Fact-checkVerified: Bill C-20 (Build Canada Homes Act), sponsored by Housing Minister Gregor Robertson, has passed the House and is at committee study in the Senate (referred June 16, 2026) - not yet law. It establishes Build Canada Homes, a federal Crown corporation to finance and build affordable housing (notably on federal land) using low-interest loans, equity and factory-built methods. Critics note it does not explicitly commit to social housing or fully address municipal cost drivers.
